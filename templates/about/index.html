HermitBook - Follow your favorite hermits through all the seasons!

Follow hermits! Catch up on seasons! See an awesome interaction with a new hermit? Easily add them without losing your place!

This started from the frustration of not being able to easily watch hermit videos in order across several channels. For instance, suppose I am a fan of Grian and Scar, and I'm bouncing between their channels trying to watch them in order. Then I watch a particularly funny video where they convince BDubs to give up his own head. Without this site I would have to find BDubs channel, find the HC playlist, and scroll through until I found the right video (hopefully its obvious from the thumbnail).

Now I can just select BDubs in the side column and it loads automatically. The site will even remember my position in the playlists so adding several dozen videos won't cause me to lose my place.

Honestly I'm unsure what else to write here as I just coded this over a holiday weekend. Ask me questions over on github or reddit.
